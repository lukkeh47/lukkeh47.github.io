Building Map

  • Updated yesterday

On this page there will be a map containing all of the engineering buildings and other buildings around it where the user can view each building's hours of operation. To view the hours of operation a user will be able to mouse over the building they are interested in and a box will appear next to it showing their hours of operation and whether or not they are currently open or closed. This will allow users to find out which buildings are available for studying and are otherwise operational for their use.
