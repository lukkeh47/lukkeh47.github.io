Zachry Engineering Education Complex

Opening Summer 2018

Amenities

Active Learning Studios

There are many meeting, study, and gathering spots within the Engineering Education Complex. The Active Learning Studios encourage study, growth, and communication among students, and the studios will include a state-of-the-art agile development collaboration center.

Starbucks

The Engineering Education Complex will include the largest Starbucks on the Texas A&M campus including an extended menu

Green Roof/Terrace

A Terrace area housing plants and gardens will act as a "Green Roof" and include a walking area with seating and a watering system created by engineering students on campus.

E-Quad

The Complex will feature an E-Quad with ample walking space, outdoor seating, and hang out areas for students to spend time and take sufficient breaks during the day.
